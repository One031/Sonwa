My Baker

From a very young age, I found comfort in baking cakes, biscuits and other favorites from my grans old recipe book after school and on weekends. Little did I know that this childhood hobby would later become my passion and a career. I completed a psychology degree and an e-marketing course and went on to work at a food magazine called avocado. Sadly the magazine closed down in 2011 but I was left with an awakened passion for food. And that is when Sonwa's Treats was born. I did a wedding cake course with the South African Wedding School in 2012 so I amm qualified in wedding cake techniques such as piping art, sugar roses and brush embroidery. I also attended a three month pastry course in New York and returned with an obsession with learning more. I have pledged to attend as many different cake related classes and courses as possible, locally and abroad (like I need an excuse to travel!!) So here I am, creating my own style of cake baking and decorating, hoping that you will all enjoy eating my cakes as much as I enjoy making them for you! Thanks for being part of my baking journey
